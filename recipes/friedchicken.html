Fried Chicken Chops

Description:

This a classic fried chicken recipe with chicken chops instead of chicken wings.

The reason i'm going with chops is because chicken chops are more meaty and has more fat in it.

Ingredients:

  • One kilogram of chicken chop
  • Salt and pepper
  • One cup of all-purpose flour
  • 500 ml. of vegetable oil
  • 2 eggs

Steps:

  1. Beat eggs in a shallow dish or bowl.
  2. Place all-purpose flour, salt and pepper in a sealed bag and shake it for it to mix.
  3. Take the chops and dredge them in the bowl of eggs one by one.
  4. After dredging, put them into sealed bag and shake it for them to absorbe the mixture.
  5. Heat oil in a deep-fryer or large saucepan to 175 degrees celcius.
  6. Fry chicken, turning frequently, until golden brown and juices run clear, 15 to 20 minutes.

Voila! Enjoy your crispy chicken chops!
